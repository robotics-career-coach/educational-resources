# ROS 2 (Robot Operating System 2)

Resources for learning the de facto standard middleware for robotics software. ROS 2 Jazzy Jalisco (released May 2024, supported through May 2029 on Ubuntu 24.04) is the current LTS to target. Per OSRF (reported by The Robot Report, Feb 2025), more than 80% of the ROS community has upgraded to ROS 2, and all of ROS 1 (including Noetic) reached end-of-life on May 23, 2025.

## Resources

- **ROS 2 Documentation — Tutorials (Jazzy/Humble/Rolling)** — Open Robotics
  https://docs.ros.org/en/jazzy/Tutorials.html
  The canonical, step-by-step beginner tutorials covering CLI tools, nodes, topics, services, and the C++/Python client libraries; the single most important reference for the cohort.

- **Programming for Robotics – ROS** — ETH Zurich Robotic Systems Lab (Marco Hutter et al.)
  https://rsl.ethz.ch/education-students/lectures/ros.html
  A university course with free downloadable slides and YouTube lecture recordings that teaches ROS architecture, packages, simulation, and TF hands-on.

- **Robotics & ROS 2 Essentials** — Henki Robotics / University of Eastern Finland
  https://github.com/henki-robotics/robotics_essentials_ros2
  An open-source 2024 master's-level course repo teaching ROS 2 + Gazebo basics through sequential hands-on exercises, actively maintained and shipping ready-to-use Docker images.

- **Getting Started with ROS 2** — Ubuntu / Canonical
  https://ubuntu.com/tutorials/getting-started-with-ros-2
  A gentle conceptual introduction to nodes and the ROS graph using turtlesim, requiring no programming experience.

- **ROS2 Tutorials** — The Robotics Back-End (Edouard Renard)
  https://roboticsbackend.com/category/ros2/
  A large library of free, beginner-focused written tutorials on Python/C++ nodes, topics, services, and parameters, with companion free videos.

# Mechanical Engineering & CAD for Robotics

Resources for 3D modeling, parametric design, and the mechanical fundamentals behind robot structures.

## Resources

- **FreeCAD** — The FreeCAD Project (open source)
  https://www.freecad.org/
  A free, open-source parametric 3D modeler with FEA, CAM, and a robot-simulation workbench; fully free with no commercial restrictions.

- **FreeCAD Documentation / Wiki** — The FreeCAD Project
  https://wiki.freecad.org/
  The official, thorough documentation covering every workbench, widely regarded as more complete than most paid courses.

- **Onshape Learning Center** — Onshape (PTC)
  https://learn.onshape.com/
  Free structured online training courses for the cloud-based, browser-native parametric CAD tool that has a free non-commercial plan.

- **Tinkercad** — Autodesk
  https://www.tinkercad.com/
  A free, browser-based, drag-and-drop 3D design tool ideal for absolute beginners and quick prototyping for 3D printing.

- **Modern Robotics (mechanics chapters)** — Kevin Lynch & Frank Park, Northwestern University
  https://hades.mech.northwestern.edu/index.php/Modern_Robotics
  The free preprint textbook covering configuration space, kinematics, and dynamics fundamentals essential to mechanical robot design.
